RUles when using symbols
1. Never start a sentence with a symbol
2. Separate symbols not in a list with words
3. Except when writing logic, avoid if, for all symbols
4. Carefully use i.e and eg
5. Don't mix words and integers inappropriately
6. Avoid using random symbols like f when sstating a theorem
7. Explain the meaning of every symbol you use.
8. Use frozen symbols properly.
9. Use consistent symbols.

Common phrases in Math
1. We will now show...
2. Avoid clearly, obviously, certainly, of course
3. Use if-then, not since-then
4. CApitalize Theorem and Lemma
